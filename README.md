# Just Pick Already 

> Stop overthinking. Just pick already.

A free, no-login 2D browser game where you walk a stickman through 5 themed zones, make choices in dilemma scenarios, and get an AI-generated personality profile at the end.

No accounts. No data sold. Completely anonymous.

**Live at [justpickalready.in](https://justpickalready.in)**

---

## How it works

Walk through 5 zones — Social Situations, Relationships, Career & Ambition, Moral Grey Areas, and Impulse vs Logic. Each zone throws scenario-based questions at you. At the end, AI reads all your choices and hands you a personality breakdown.

## Stack

Next.js · TypeScript · Tailwind CSS · Shadcn · Framer Motion · Zustand · Supabase · Groq (Llama 3) · Vercel
